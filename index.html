Hi I'm Ali Ramadhan, currently a graduate student pursuing an accelerated MSc with the Department of Physics and Astronomy at the University of Waterloo. I am interested in practical applications of ultrafast lasers to molecular physics, materials science, photonics, and the biological sciences. In the lab I use ultrafast lasers to study molecular dynamics, create molecular movies, synthesize new molecules, and form new materials, among other stuff. Outside of the lab I am passionate about teaching and this term I am excited to merge my love for food and cooking with teaching thermodynamics! I enjoy cooking new dishes while mastering old ones and road tripping across the country when the laser stops working.
